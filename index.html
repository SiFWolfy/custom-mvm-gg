SiF's Overclocks!

This page is intended to list every single stat change that SiF did in an attempt to make useless weapons slightly more bearable in a sense.

Scout

Primary

Scattergun

Overclock A - "Scattercannon" ($400)

> Massive increase in burst damage, less uptime.

  • +250% bullets per shot

  • -66% damage penalty

  • -85% clip size

  • -15% slower reload time

Overclock B - "Uplifter" ($200)

> Trades early game damage for better credit magnet and overall sustain.

  • +50% more accurate

  • +80% credits magnet radius

  • +25% health from credit

  • -40% bullets per shot

  • -15% slower reload time

Baby Face's Blaster

Overclock A - "Public Enemy" ($400)

> Run faster than average, but can't jump.

  • On Hit: Gain speedboost

  • +10% bullets per shot

  • -30% damage penalty

  • -34% clip size

  • 30% slower move speed

  • No double jump

Overclock B - "Dillinger Dispatcher" ($200)

> Run faster when hurt too, but it decays.

  • When Hurt: Gain speedboost

  • +34% clip size

  • +40% more accurate

  • +40% faster firing speed

  • 35% slower reload speed

  • 20% slower move speed

  • Boost decays over time

Back Scatter

Overclock A - "Recon Ambush" ($400)

> Backshot will crit and pieece resists, but weak upfront.

  • On Back Hit: 100% critical hit

  • +10% bullets per shot

  • -20% more accurate

  • Attacks pierce damage resistances

  • -10% bullets per shot

  • -20% slower reload time

Overclock B - "Lever Rifle" ($200)

> Headshot deals high damage, though It's all or nothing.

  • On Headshot: 100% critical hit

  • Successive shots become more accurate

  • -67% clip size

  • -90% bullets per shot
